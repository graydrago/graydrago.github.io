I'v used C++11, SDL, GLEW, cmake, WebGL and of course emscripten for building this example. WASD is used for camera moving and mouse for looking around (just click on a scene to grab focus).
1 - toggle to a FPS camera
2 - toggle to a flying camera
shift - move faster
left mouse button - paint a polygon
